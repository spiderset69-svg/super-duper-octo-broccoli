Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Hijama therapy.

Is Hijama painful?

Most people do not find Hijama painful. The sensation is often described as a slight pinch or pressure, similar to a deep tissue massage. We ensure the process is as comfortable as possible for you.

How long does a session take?

A typical session lasts between 30 to 45 minutes. This includes consultation, preparation, the cupping procedure, and post-treatment care.

Is it safe and hygienic?

Absolutely. We adhere to the strictest hygiene standards. All equipment, including cups and blades, is single-use and sterile. Your safety is our utmost priority.

How often should I get Hijama?

The frequency depends on your individual health goals. For general wellness, once every 3-4 months is recommended. For specific conditions, we will create a personalized treatment plan during your consultation.
